Use of Blockchain technology

We use blockchain technology for sharing and transacting of documents between the organizations and its stakeholders in our real-time cloud services. This allows to give a complete track of all the documents for further verification and security issues.

We entrust security of all approvals or disapprovals via high degree of encryption by use of .pfx files and QR code. All the authentication processes are thus digitized under a highly secure technology.
